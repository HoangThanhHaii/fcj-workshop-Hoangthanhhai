Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [Onboarding, Company Orientation & AWS Account Setup](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [AWS Core Services Overview & AI Environment Setup](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [CV Structure Analysis & Text Extraction Research](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [Backend Architecture, API Routing & Amazon Bedrock Research](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [Prompt Engineering & Scoring Rubric Design](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [Core Features Systematization & Deployment Planning](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [End-to-End AI Workflow Mapping & JSON Contract Definition](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [CV Text Parsing Workflow & Extraction Checklist Setup](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [CV Analysis Prompt Development & JSON Output Stabilization](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [Interview Question Generation & Overall Scoring Logic](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [AWS Voice Services Integration & Fallback Scenario Setup](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12:** [AI Quality Assurance, Prompt Fine-Tuning & Demo Finalization](1.12-week12/)
